Rubber Ducky

Your helper in any tough situation. Just explain your problem, and you will find a solution!

What is it?

Rubber Ducky is your helper in any tough situation. Are you desperate for a solution to your problem? Searching for an answer for a long time? Lost in a huge amount of information? You probably haven't tried talking to Rubber Ducky! Just explain your problem in detail... and you will find a solution! Magic? No. It's Rubber Ducky!

How to use it?

  • Be as specific as possible, or Ducky might not understand you.
  • Don’t leave out details, even the sensitive ones. This duck’s memory is not very good anyway.
  • Talk to Ducky like a human would talk to a human. Ducks have feelings too!
  • While explaining your problem, you will definitely find some useful insights.
  • While reading through your writing, you might even find a complete solution.
  • If nothing still comes to your mind, you can go to a walk and then return to your problem with the fresh head. All your notes will be saved.
